Займався боксом, легкою атлетикою, любив погуляти. Ходив на додаткові, займався інтернет бізнесом. A young German couple is stuck in the gray of everyday life. They want adventure, a starry sky over their heads, but all around is just a routine of workdays. So they decide to give up their usual lives and seek happiness on a desert island. At first things are going well: barbecue and exotic fruits, a hammock on the beach and time alone. Only the ideal life begins to collapse quickly, because you will not hide from yourself, even on a desert island.

Навчаюсь в Прикарпатському національному університеті імені В. Стефаника на факультеті комп'ютерні науки. Ray Kroc, the narrator of this story, proves by his own life example that it is never too late to change your life. He was 52 years old when he met the McDonald's brothers and opened his first franchise at their establishment. It is a story of trial and error, a success story of a man who, despite all the difficulties of life, was able to reach the top in his field.
